Understanding Impact Wrench Specifications

RPM (Revolutions Per Minute)

RPM indicates the rotational speed of the anvil. Multiple speed modes allow low RPM for precise fastening and higher RPM for rapid tightening or removal of fasteners.

IPM (Impacts Per Minute)

IPM is the number of hammer blows delivered per minute. Higher IPM helps loosen rusted or high-torque fasteners and improves driving efficiency in tough applications.

Torque (Nm)

Torque is the rotational force applied to the fastener. Max fastening torque indicates tightening capability; some models also list higher nut-busting torque for removal performance.
