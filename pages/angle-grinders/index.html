Understanding Angle Grinder Specifications

RPM (Revolutions Per Minute)

RPM indicates the rotational speed of the grinding disc. Higher RPM values enable faster material removal and smoother finishes on metal surfaces.

Disc Size

The disc diameter determines the depth of cut and the surface area available for grinding. Common sizes include 115mm, 125mm, and 230mm, with larger discs providing greater cutting depth and coverage.

Spindle Thread

The spindle thread size (typically M10 or M14) determines which accessories and discs are compatible with the tool. Ensure your chosen accessories match the spindle specification.
